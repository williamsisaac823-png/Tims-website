ENGINEERED ADVANTAGE

Curved Lower Beam

The JDB's curved lower beam makes it easy to bend and build virtually anything you need for your building. Complex geometries become easy and efficient — letting you and your team meet your customers' high expectations, and deliver on time and within budget.

The JDB's flexibility unlocks more possible profiles with varying dimensions — thanks to the 269° front free bending space and the largest rear free space under the upper jaw of any machine in its class.

  • ✓Maximum bending angle makes all the difference
  • ✓You decide the gripper-arm placement to meet your needs
  • ✓Dynamic positioning for more accuracy
  • ✓Open space inside of the tooling
  • ✓Amazing clamping pressure for more accuracy
  • ✓Bendex inside!
  • ✓Jorns conveying solutions to meet each customer's needs
  • ✓Best of all — automate in phases as you grow: prep for side infeed, add JPM when you're ready

The ability to retreat the bending beam more than 11 inches creates enormous free space. Multiple gripping solutions — gripping positions on the material, vacuum grippers, and short-part clamps — let the machine run your profiles automatically without any custom add-on.

The optional high-speed slitter, covering 10 ft/sec, is essential for tapered profiles. The exchangeable slitter head with rollform tooling adds even more flexibility — all in one complete system.

MODERN TECHNOLOGY FOR TODAY'S CHALLENGES

Today's technology is evident throughout the Jorns JDB: a powerful graphic control with auto-sequencing, zoned gripping solutions paired with automated infeed tables so a single operator can produce multiple parts at once, dynamic positioning, optional laser-hardened tooling, a networkable machine tool, remote diagnostics via TeamViewer, and the powerful B&R Automation Suite for servicing. Jorns is prepared for today's needs.

SAFETY, STANDARD

A light curtain protects the operator during automatic operation, with laser monitoring of the beams when work is needed inside the protected area, plus modern safety fencing — all standard. The auto-infeed tables make loading flat sheet safer and easier, and the back gauge can operate close to the bending moment in fully automatic mode, so the operator has no material handling to do until the profile is finished.

When you weigh all the facts, Jorns gives you a high-performing, accurate, reliable, technically advanced, safe, automated bending machine — positioning you for today's and tomorrow's business.
