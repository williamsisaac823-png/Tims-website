SMS Notification Program

Authorization statement — last updated: July 2026

This page documents how consent was obtained for the single phone number enrolled in Tim White Machinery's SMS notification program, for the purpose of A2P 10DLC campaign review.

Who is enrolled

This program has exactly one recipient: Tim White, the owner and operator of Tim White Machinery. No customers, leads, or members of the public are enrolled in or receive messages through this program.

How consent was given

Tim White personally requested and authorized his own mobile number to be enrolled in this notification system when it was built for his business. He asked his website developer to set up automatic text alerts to his own phone whenever a visitor submits the contact form at metalfabmachines.com, so he can respond to new sales inquiries quickly. This is an internal operational tool for his own business use, not a marketing or promotional messaging list, and not open to public sign-up.

Program details

  • Message frequency varies based on inquiry volume.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out of messages.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Mobile numbers used in connection with this program are never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes.
